Fun Facts

giraffes...

  • are the tallest living land animal
  • have excellent eye sight
  • are herbivores - eat only plants
  • are very social and roam in groups called "towers"

Why I love Giraffes

Although lanky and very tall, they move with grace and elegance. They wear a naturally beautiful fur coat with a fantastic irregular-edged pattern. They spend most of their time eating a vegetarian diet and are not religated to the "low hanging fruit" as they are able to reach heights that most other herbivores cannot. They are free to roam the African savannas and woodlands and are very social creatures.
